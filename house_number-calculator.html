House Number Sum

Welcome to the House Number Sum calculator! This tool helps you find the single-digit sum of a house number. Enter the house number below and the result will be calculated automatically.

Example: If you enter the house number "1234", the sum of its digits will be calculated as "1 + 2 + 3 + 4 = 10" and then "1 + 0 = 1".
